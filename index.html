Who We Are

We are a Christian community of believers. We are non-denominational and not tied to any denomination but interdenominational organization. We believe that it is possible to recognize the gifts given by God, to develop them further and to grow in them in order to be able to serve others.

What We Stand For

Our vision is to transform lives through God's unconditional love and equip people to fulfill His purpose. Our mission is to build a family of believers where Jesus is visible, and God's works are manifested through training, outreach, and community projects.

What We Do

We offer weekly meetings, small groups, prayer meetings, conferences, training & education workshops, personal guidance, mission outreach and share encouraging words via podcast among others

Why You Should Join Us

We believe that every person is blessed by God with talents, abilities, relationships, gifts, experiences and resources that he or she can use for his or her own benefit. This is the reason why you can join us on this mission

Our Mission

Mission: To create a platform that enables us to build a family of believers in which Jesus becomes visible and the greater works of God are done. This task is to be fulfilled through training, missionary work, practical supports, family meetings, prayer outreaches, projects works in communities, etc.

Our Vision

Vision: To change lives through the unconditional love (agape love) of God and to equip people for this task[As Paul writes in Ephesians 3:13-21, "Therefore I ask you not to lose heart at my tribulations on your behalf, for they are your glory." Through the power of God's love, we are strengthened to carry out this mission, helping others grow in faith and fulfilling their purpose in Christ...]
